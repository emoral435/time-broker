title: time-broker

Get back your time. Use a managed broker, hooked up to your calendar provider.

time-broker is a TUI-first command-line tool that connects to your calendar
provider (Google Calendar, Fantastical, etc.) so you can see your availability
and book timeslots directly from the terminal.

## Supported Providers

- **Google Calendar**
