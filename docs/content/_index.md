Get back your time. Use a managed broker, hooked up to your calendar provider.

time-broker is a TUI-first command-line tool that connects to your calendar
provider (Google Calendar, Fantastical, etc.) so you can see your availability
and book timeslots directly from the terminal.

time-broker was made for the specific purpose of easily getting timeslots throughout the week of when I am available. For instance, have you ever had to manually go to your calendar provider to find the timeslots within the week of when you are free just to book a 1:1 or an interviewer? time-broker automates this process in a hassle-free and seamless way, with frictionless integration with all your favorite calendar providers all contained within the terminal so that your hands never have to leave the keyboard.

## Supported Providers

- **Google Calendar**
